The old concrete fountain has not run in years. Rather than fix the pump, I'm
turning it into a small wildlife pond: shallow shelves for marginals, a deeper
middle for oxygenators, and a boggy corner where the overflow used to sit.

Today was mostly demolition and measuring. Next up is sealing the cracks and
working out the planting shelves.
