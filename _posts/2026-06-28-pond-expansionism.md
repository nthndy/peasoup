I can do it in a 42L bucket, so why not a 3600L chlorine-bleached communal fountain? It can't possibly be that difficult.

![Communal fountain with chlorine tablet stains]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/fountain_chlorine.jpg)

The basic idea is to take this sterile environment and convert it into a self-cleaning, biologically balanced wildlife pond, replete with a variety of native plants and minibeasts that will hopefully help encourage other wildlife into the already beautiful communal garden. A few conversations were had, and before I knew it people were keen and the fountain was emptied, scrubbed and refilled. Now the tricky bit begins.

![The communal fountain, emptied and scrubbed]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/fountain_clean.jpg)
